Tír na nÓg Bed & Breakfast is a luxurious 5 star Bed & Breakfast located close to the Dublin City Centre and also situate near the shores of Blessington Street Basin lake. This comfortable country manor house was built by Lady Elizabeth in 1912. It is an ambience of graceful elegance and heritage with modern comforts while retaining an old world enchantment with our collection of antique furnishings and paintings. Tír na nÓg Bed & Breakfast provides a timeless experiences. to guests.
